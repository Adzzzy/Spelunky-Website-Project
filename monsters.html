An Assortment Of

Spelunky's Monsters

Snake: A simple enemy that slithers back and forth and can be killed easily. Found in the mines.

Cobra: A snake which can spit from a distance, and knock you down. Found in the mines.

Bat: The bat flies towards the spelunker if they get too close. Found in the mines.

Spider: A small enemy that clings on the ceiling, and falls down on any spelunker that walks underneath.

Spinner Spider: A spider that lowers itself with its web at varying intervals. Found in the mines.

Giant Spider: A massive spider that can spit out cobwebs. When killed it drops jewels and Sticky Paste.

Skeleton: What first seems like a pile of bones pops out into a skeleton when anyone gets near.

Scorpion: An agile monster that lunges forward and strikes when the spelunker is in view.

Caveman: He's just a simple caveman who roams the caves, but don't upset him or he'll get mad!

Tiki-man: Similar to the caveman, but he wears a face mask to hide his ugly face.

Blue Frog: A frog that jumps around irrationally towards the player. Found in the jungle.

Orange Frog: Exactly the same as his blue frog counterpart, but instead he detonates to deal extra damage.

Giant Frog: A big, fat frog that spits out smaller blue frogs. Found in the jungle.

Mantrap: This pirahnna-plant-looking fellow will eat you in one bite if you walk into him.

Pirahnna: This evil fish stays in water and bites any spelunkers that it comes in contact with.

Killer Bee: A bee that will erratically fly around in your direction, stinging you like mad. Only found in the jungle's beehive.

Queen Bee: A massive bee that flies around erratically. Drops Royal Jelly when killed.

Snail: A small, slow critter that spits out poisonous bubbles that float upwards.

Monkey: An annoying monkey that will cling onto your back and steal your items and gold, occasionally knocking you out as well.

Jiang-Shi: A jumping, undead enemy, has a similar movement to the frog, but jumps much higher.

Vampire: The Vampire first clings onto walls like a bat, before flying down towards the player and jumping around spastically.

Yeti: Simply standing around minding its own business, a yeti will throw you away if you come in contact.

Yeti King: A big yeti that can shout to make large icicles fall from the ceiling, knocking the player out.

Mammoth: A mammoth that walks around the ice caves, shooting ice bullets that freeze the spelunker into an ice block.

Alien: A small alien that comes out of a UFO, it will hop around aimlessly.

UFO: A flying saucer that shoots slow lasers downwards towards the player. When destroyed, causes an explosion and spits out an alien.

Hawkman: Similar in design to the caveman, but instead of just running around, it will jump around at high speeds.

Crocman: Similar to the Hawkman in behavior, but will also teleport around the place, occasionally into walls.

Magma man: Comes out of lava as a fiery, walking humanoid. Several can spawn out of one lava pool.

Scorpion Fly: Flies around like a Killer Bee, but takes several hits to kill.

Mummy: A very slow, big enemy that spits out a swarm of harmful locusts in an area in front of him.

Anubis: A high-health, dangerous enemy that wields the sceptre, conjuring glowing purple death rings. Can travel through walls.

Olmec: The game's boss, defeat him to win. He crushes the ground beneath him and spawns smaller enemies. Best Olmec by making him fall into lava.

Vlad: An upgrade from the vampire. Will drop Vlad's cape or Vlad's Amulet when killed.

Imp: A small, flying devil. Can hold a cauldron of lava to drop on your head.

Devil: A blue devil that has spikes on his head. Will ram into you with his head if you're in range, or can jump up into you from below.

Succubus: An evil hag that disguises herself as the damsel, but then pops out to kill you.

Yama: The secret boss of the game, Yama is extremely hard to reach and beat. You need to destroy his two fists, and then his head, unlocking the final cutscene.
