An Assortment Of

Spelunky's Monsters

Snake: A simple enemy that slithers back and forwards and can be killed easily. Found in the caves.

Cobra: A snake which can spit from a distance, and knock you down. Found in the caves.

Bat:The bat flies towards the spelunker if they get too close. Found in the caves.

Spider:A small enemy that clings on the celing, and falls down on any spelunker that walks underneath.

Spinner Spider:A spider that lowers itself on it's web on designated intervals. Found in the caves.

Giant Spider:A massive spider that can spit out cobwebs. When killed it drops jewels and Sticky Paste.

Skeleton: What first seems like a pile of bones pops out into a skeleton when anyone gets near.

Scorpion: A fast monster that strikes forward with speed when the spelunker is in view.

Caveman: He's just a simple caveman who roams the caves, but don't anger him or he'll get mad!.

Tiki-man: Similar to the caveman, but he wears a face mask to hide his ugly face.

Blue Frog: A frog that jumps around irrationally towards the player. Found in the jungle.

Orange Frog: Exactly the same as his blue frog counterpart, but instead he detonates to deal extra damage.

Giant Frog: A big fat frog that spits out smaller blue frogs. Found in the jungle.

Mantrap: This pirahnna plant looking fellow will eat you in one bite if you walk into him.

Pirahnna:This evil fish stays in water and bites any humans that it comes in contact with.

Killer Bee: A bee that will randomly fly around in your direction, stinging you like mad. Only found in the jungle's beehive.

Queen Bee: A massive bee that flies around randomly. Drops royal jelly when killed.

Snail: A small slow critter that spits out poisonous bubbles that float upwards.

Monkey: An annoying monkey will cling on your back and steal your items and gold, occasionally knocking you out as well.

Jiang-Shi:A jumping undead enemy, has a similar movement to the frog, but jumps much higher.

Vampire: The Vampire first clings off walls like a bat, flies down towards the player and then jumpes around spastically.

Yeti: Simply standing around minding its own business, a yeti will throw you away if you come in contact.

Yeti King: A big yeti that can shout to make ice shards fall from the sky, knocking out the player.

Mammoth: A mammoth that walks around the ice caves, shooting ice bullets that freeze the spelunker into and ice block

Alien: A small alien that comes out of a UFO, it will hop around aimlessly.

UFO: A flying object that shoots slow lasers downards towards the plater. When destroyed causes an explosion and spits out an alien.

Hawkman: Similar in design to the caveman, but instead of running around it will also jump around with high speed.

Crocman: Similar to the Hawkman in behavior, but will also teleport around the place, occasionally into walls.

Magma man: Comes out of lava as a walking Magma Man. Several of these can spawn out of one lava place.

Scorpion Fly: Flies around like a Killer Bee, but takes several hits to kill.

Mummy: A very slow, big enemy that spits out a swarm of dangeous locust in an area in front of him.

Anubis: A high-health dangerous enemy that wields the sceptre, that spits out glowing purple death rings. Can travel through walls.

Olmec: The games boss, killing Olmec will win you the game. He jumps around and spawns smaller enemies, and you can kill him by making hi fall into lava.

Vlad: An upgrade from the vampire, will drop Vlad's cape or Vlad's Amulet when killed.

Imp: A small flying devil, can hold a cauldron of lava to drop on your head.

Devil: A blue devil that has spikes on his head. Will ram into you with his head if your in range, or can jump up into you from the bottom.

Succubus: An evil hag that disguises herself as the damsel, but then pops out to kill you.

Yama: The secret boss of the game, Yama is extremely hard to get too and beat. You need to blow up his too fists, and then his head. This will trigger a final last cutscene
