A Collection Of

Spelunky's Items

Rope Pile: Contains three ropes which can be deployed to help reach high places.

Bomb bag: Contains three bombs which can be used to destroy terrain and damage enemies.

Bomb Box: Contains 12 bombs.

Spectacles: Allow the players to see gems and items in the wall. Also allows for a greater field of view in dark levels.

Climbing Gloves: Allows the player to cling to walls.

Pitcher's Mitt: Greatly strengthens the player's throw.

Spring Shoes: Allow the player to jump higher.

Spike Shoes: Damage to enemies via jumping on them is increased.

Paste: Makes bombs sticky so they can cling to walls and enemies.

Compass: Creates a red arrow that points towards the exit.

Mattock: Can be used to mine through blocks when held by the player.

Boomerang: Travels a short distance when thrown, damaging enemies along the way before returning to the player.

Machete: Deals more damage than the standard whip. Can also cut through cobwebs.

Crysknife: A weapon that replaces the whip, it is found exclusively in the worm. Kills enemies in a single swing with the exception of a few select foes.

Webgun: Shoots out webs that can slow enemies or be used for climbing.

Plasma Cannon: A weapon found exclusively on the Alien Mothership. Has huge recoil and can destroy terrain.

Camera: Stuns most enemies for a short period of time. Can kill certain enemies such as undead enemies and bats.

Teleporter: Teleports the player a random number of tiles when used. Extremely unpredictable and kills the player if they teleport into a wall.

Parachute: Activates automatically when a player is falling from a dangerous height to prevent fall damage.

Cape: Allows the player to float midair, preventing fall-damage and allowing for a safe landing on spikes.

Jetpack: Allows the player to fly until fuel runs out. Fuel replenishes when the player lands.

Shield: Found on the black knight enemy in the haunted castle. When held, the player can crush enemies and deflect projectiles.

Royal Jelly: It is dropped by the queen bee when killed. It gives the player 4 hearts.

Idol: An item that commonly spawns in most levels. It is surrounded by traps dependent on the area the idol is found in. Its value increases by 5000 in each area.

Kapala: The second reward received from Kali after enough sacrifices at her altar. It allows you to collect blood from enemies which is turned into hearts.

Udjat Eye: Found in the mines after opening a chest using the gold key. It allows you to see items in the wall and it blinks to help the player find the Black Market.

Ankh: It revives the player when they die. If used on the same stage as the Moai Head, the player will receive the Hedjet.

Hedjet: Received when the player gets revived in the Moai head. Can open the door to the City of Gold if the Sceptre is held.

Sceptre: Wielded by Anubis in the first level of the Temple. Shoots purple rings which home onto enemies and can travel through walls.

Book of the dead: Found in the City of Gold. It opens the door to hell, and moves as the player nears the entrance to help them locate it.
