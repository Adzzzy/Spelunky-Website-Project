ALL ABOUT US


WHO WE ARE

Our names are Adam and Jonasz. One of our favourite hobbies is playing games. It is through this hobby, that we discovered Spelunky. Once we started playing Spelunky we were hooked! Unlike most games, Spelunky doesn't only require time investment, but skill gained from playing it over and over and learning from past mistakes.

When we finally beat Spelunky, we didn't stop playing like we'd normally do with most games, we took it to the next level. We started making our own Spelunky projects, such as custom maps, as well as this informative website you see before you!
